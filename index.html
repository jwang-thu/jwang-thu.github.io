Research Interest

  • Fast algorithms.
  • Numerical quadratures.
  • Automatic mesh refinement.
  • Integral equation method for elliptic and parabolic partial differential equations.
  • Numerical methods for the simulation of diffusion and Stokesian flows.

  • Explicit unconditionally stable methods for the heat equation via potential theory, A. Barnett, C.L. Epstein, L. Greengard, S. Jiang, J. Wang. (arxiv)
  • On the accurate evaluation of unsteady Stokes layer potentials in moving two-dimensional geometries, L. Greengard, S. Jiang, J. Wang. (arxiv)
  • Fast integral equation methods for linear and semilinear heat equations in moving domains, J. Wang, L. Greengard, S. Jiang, S. Veerapaneni. (arxiv)
  • An integral equation method for the simulation of doubly-periodic suspensions of rigid bodies in a shearing viscous flow, J. Wang, A. Barnett, E. Nazockdast. (arxiv)
